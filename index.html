What MakerShare does!

During COVID-19, many essential workers lack access to basic medical supplies like masks. Community members have found alternative DIY methods to stay safe, like creating masks with old t-shirts! MakerShare is a platform where essential workers can request supplies from community members, and community members are rewarded and recognized for their donations through a friendly leaderboard style competition.

Location-based Service

Work with those closest to you through our location-tracking service.

Friendly Competition

Donate more medical supplies and watch your name rise up the leaderboards!

Essential Worker?

MakerShare is a multipurpose platform. Community members and essential workers collaborate in multiple ways to meet unexpected needs the COVID-19 virus has created.

Post What You Need

Post, and detail what product you need community members to create! Posting will allow the community to support you as you fight on the front lines against this virus.

Receive Support You Didn't Know You Needed

Community members will post products and services they can provide for you! These posts will contain the distance their location and services are from you; receive product from those closest to you, so you can spend the most time battling COVID-19.

Community Member?

MakerShare is a multipurpose platform. Community members and essential workers collaborate in multiple ways to meet unexpected needs the COVID-19 virus has created.

Post What You Need

Create, and post what you have to give. Essential workers will respond to your posts, accepting your generous offers.

Provide Exactly What Essential Workers Need

Essential Workers will post what products and services they need. These posts will contain the distance the essential worker is from you; work with those closest to ensure their needs are met as fast as possible.

Transporting Goods/Services

After an essential worker and community member pair up to provide goods for the essential worker, how the goods/services are transported is up to the group! The essential worker can either drive to pick up the goods/services, or the community member can deliver them.
